JENGAPAY

Official Platform Overview & Mission

JENGAPAY is a next-generation decentralized digital platform designed to revolutionize the construction and logistics ecosystem across East Africa (Tanzania, Uganda, and the DRC). Powered by the Pi Network blockchain ecosystem and our native Jengapay Token (JPT), we provide a secure, fast, and transparent environment for digital escrow payments and multi-app utility.

Our Core Leadership & Team

  • Dominic Patrick – Founder & Lead Developer: The visionary behind JENGAPAY, directing the platform's overarching architecture, blockchain integration, and strategic regional deployment.
  • The JENGAPAY Core Team: A dedicated team of digital experts, software engineers, and logistics specialists collaborating to manage construction project workflows, security, and marketplace dynamics.

What We Do

  • Double-Sided Marketplace: We seamlessly bridge the gap by connecting clients and visitors with verified construction material suppliers and professional independent contractors (fundis).
  • Next-Gen AI Integration: The platform features an advanced AI Construction Advisor tool to offer real-time technical building guidance, material optimization, and automated customer advisory services.
  • Blockchain-Powered Security: By utilizing Pi Network authentication (Pi.authenticate()) and smart contracts via Jengapay Token (JPT), we secure payments and protect users from fraudulent marketplace operations.

Our Vision

To empower pioneers and builders in the region with automated, secure, and low-cost digital tools, establishing JENGAPAY as the ultimate hub for modern construction management and reliable logistics.
